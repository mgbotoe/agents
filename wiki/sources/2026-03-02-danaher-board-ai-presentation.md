## Summary

Presented "Danaher Brain" AI strategy to the board. Covered software development (GitHub Copilot to 3,000 developers, $40M+ reinvested productivity target) and legal (400 associates, $90M external legal spend, Ligura deployment). Board was highly engaged — meeting ran over scheduled 20 minutes.

## Productivity Framework

1. **Cost reduction** — delivers OP impact
2. **Reinvested productivity** — time used elsewhere (preferred framing)
3. **Unrealized productivity** — avoid (people taking longer coffee breaks)
