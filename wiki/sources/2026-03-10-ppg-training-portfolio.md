# PPG Training — AI Enterprise Portfolio Management

Granola meeting: `25611957-47fd-450b-8fc3-177383ee90dc`

## Summary

Karen Bogren (DBSO) led PPG implementation training. PPG = Product Planning Group — monthly decision-making forum for portfolio execution. Created by Hawk (now Fortive) in early 2000s. Goal: drive sustainable AI impact through lean portfolio management.

## Core Principles

- Decision-making forum, NOT status reporting
- Pull system vs push: only initiate when capacity available
- Target 70–75% allocation toward strategy-linked projects
- Bad multitasking = 30–40% productivity loss per context switch

## Three-Tier Governance

1. Portfolio Daily Management — tactical 30-day decisions
2. PPG — monthly execution decisions and barrier removal
3. SIR (Strategic Innovation Review) — strategic oversight

## AI Org Maturity

- Currently: Basic (execution visibility)
- Next: Intermediate (Cost Benefit Ratio across portfolio)
- Future: Advanced (enterprise optimization, OPC alignment)
