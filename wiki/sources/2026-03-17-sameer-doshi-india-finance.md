# Danaher India Finance Use Cases — Sameer Doshi

Granola meeting: `2e1ba4d3-db6c-4b6c-bba6-9eceaf143beb`

## Summary

Meeting with Sameer Doshi (CFO Danaher India, 15 years). Five finance use cases reviewed. Life Sciences platform live, Diagnostics going live. Shift from OpCo to platform approach.

## Use Cases

1. **Sales Partner Channel Reconciliation** — Phase 1 complete, pre-production. Awaiting ARB approval. Saves 2–3 hours per cycle. Scalable across 95–99% of $200M Diagnostics India.
2. **Airline Invoice GST Reconciliation** — $2M annual loss potential. CWT vs airline invoice reconciliation for GST credits. Scalable across 8 legal entities.
3. **AP Invoice Processing** — Automation across approval chain. Phase 3: data optimization.
4. **Expense Claims Validation** — AI fraud detection. Challenge: multiple P&E tools (Concur, Zoho, Expensify).
5. **FP&A Reporting** — Manual consolidation from OneStream. Timing improvement needed.

## Key Points

- Only Use Case 2 has quantified ROI ($2M)
- New PPG process requires business case for resource allocation
- OneStream as single source of truth enables global scaling
