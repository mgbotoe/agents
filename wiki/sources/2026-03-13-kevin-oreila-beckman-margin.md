# Kevin Oreila — Beckman Margin Expansion Strategy Review

## Summary

8-week McKinsey diagnostic: $50–80M run-rate margin opportunity at Beckman (US only). 4K+ files processed, 50M+ data rows, 1.4K+ contracts assessed, 30+ stakeholders interviewed.

## Three Pricing Levers

- **Price setting**: Tail SKU optimization for low-velocity products
- **Price getting**: Dynamic deal scoring to reduce discount variability ($17–20M)
- **Price netting**: MAC compliance enforcement + contract terms optimization

## Dynamic Deal Scoring

- 137 deals in one micro-segment showing wide discount range
- Move deals up one letter grade (F→D, D→C) = $17–20M impact
- Requires CPQ system updates

## Timeline

- Year 1 (2026): ~$7M starting April
- Year 2 (2027): $30–50M as deal scoring + MAC restructuring take effect
- Investment: 2–3 analytics resources + process changes + tech upgrades
