# Quick Sync with Chris Bouda — Copilot Training Plan

Planning session for Microsoft Copilot training rollout to finance teams, plus proposed DC visit.

## Copilot Training Plan

- Microsoft proposing ~6 training sessions targeting May completion
  - 1 beginner, 1 advanced, 2 sessions on building agents, remainder adjusted for time zones
  - "Frontier Finest" concept also being shared — Microsoft to send details
- Target audience: AI Champions / power users (not all 2,200 finance)
  - Goal: 200-300 people across finance total
  - Each app owner to nominate 10-20 power users who can support rest of team
- Requested a finance CFO to join at least one session for relatable use cases
- Two licensing scenarios:
  1. Licensed users -> full Copilot training
  2. Unlicensed users -> lighter session (can still use Copilot features without full license)
- Working to secure additional Microsoft licenses before May

## Use Case Prioritization

- Finance to get prioritized AI support — "an unfair share of AI time"
- John Bedford and counterpart to own use case prioritization and finance navigation
- Madina, Martin, and CU should bring suspected use cases rather than struggle to find contacts
- Goal: move beyond "here's a tool, play with it" toward genuinely transformational use cases
- HR AI enablement work seen as a model to replicate in finance
