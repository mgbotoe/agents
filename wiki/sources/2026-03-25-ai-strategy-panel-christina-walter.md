# AI Strategy & Career Transformation Panel

Granola meeting: `86d15c79-f892-4321-a835-d69edd6ce1b4`

## Summary

Two Geese MBA speakers at Women Who Rule AI weekly event. Madina shared career path (UC Berkeley → UCSF → healthcare informatics MS → Danaher AI PM) and practical AI implementation lessons. Christina Walter presented experimentation framework.

## Madina's Key Points

- Pivot from traditional PM to AI strategy was easier than expected
- Critical principle: "Not every use case belongs in an AI narrative"
- Comfortable rejecting inflated ROI projections
- Can build proof of concept in an afternoon vs waiting 2 sprints for engineering
- Enterprise AI team: 6 pillars, 12+ people/team, large India team
- Prototyping changes the conversation with leadership and vendors

## Expanded ROI Beyond Cost Savings

- HR setting stretch goals from increased confidence
- Finance building predictive models independently
- Marketing testing campaigns at 10x rate
- Support team learning to debug code

## Key Takeaways

- Don't need data science background to lead AI initiatives
- Must get hands-on before leading others
- Speak business terms to executives, not technical jargon
- Younger employees adopting faster — resistance becoming competitive disadvantage
