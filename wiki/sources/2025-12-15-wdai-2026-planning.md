# WDAI New Members Experience Connect — 2026 Ideas

Core team planning session for 2026 website, migration, and volunteer strategy.

## Website Migration Plan (January 2026)

- Built from scratch using Next.js + Vercel (off Wix)
- Single portal: membership, billing, resources, events, course enrollment
- Automated Stripe payment processing + member directory
- Migration steps for ~1,000 existing members:
  1. Announcement email (week of Jan 5)
  2. Manually cancel all Wix subscriptions by hand
  3. Send coupon codes: monthly members get 1 month free, annual get 1 year free
- Mailchimp integration: Active.com tag for existing, Active.org for new portal members

## 2026 Team Structure

- Monthly 30-minute sub-team meetings planned
- Deliberate volunteer recruitment vs. open-door policy
- AI Foundations instructor certification proposal:
  - Certify engaged mentors who show leadership ability
  - Can specialize in specific modules
  - Reduces dependency on Helen, Lauren, and Madina

## AI Foundations Enhancements

- Plan to run three times per year in 2026
- Certificate/portfolio concept: AI-generated personalized certificates from participant reflections
- Public showcase events for advanced participants
- New reflection system built into portal (private journaling with share option)

## Website Redesign Priorities

- Homepage doesn't clearly communicate value proposition
- Join Now page content should move to homepage
- Need clearer pricing and testimonials on main landing page
- Rita and Brigitte to provide redesign mockups by Wednesday
- Google Analytics 4 setup needed (volunteer offered to help)
