## Ideal User Flow (Sandhya's framework)

1. **Community engagement**: email -> virtual events (lurking) -> participating -> Slack (general) -> Slack (niche channels)
2. **Skill mastery**: portal/courses -> prompt templates -> custom GPTs -> advanced builds (Claude Code etc.)
- Key tension: over-automation risks losing "give, not just take" dynamic
- Brigitte: "it's too automated — we've lost the heart"
- Two personas: members who stop at AI Intermediate (still strong advocates) and those who progress to advanced building
