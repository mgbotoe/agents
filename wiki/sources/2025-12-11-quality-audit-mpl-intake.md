# Quality Audit - MPL Intake Form Alignment

Meeting with Nafiza Nurani (QA) and Siyu Wu (AI Enterprise) to review the quality audit AI project proposal.

## Key Numbers

- QR18 team: 5 auditors covering 127+ global sites (medical devices, pharma, genomics)
- Total budget: $300k over 2 years (30% AI = ~$100k for 2026, 70% master data platform)
- Expected ROI: 51% (time efficiency 20% + risk mitigation); would be 117% if people efficiency included
- One-time implementation: $236-237k
- Snowflake master data platform ready Q3 2026

## Problem

- Manual audit planning, execution, reporting across programs
- Manual FDA website mining for recalls and site histories
- Difficulty identifying repeat issues and gathering previous findings
- Risk of missing critical info before third-party audits

## Proposed AI Solution

- 5-7 year technology roadmap; Phase 3 (2026-2027) = AI audit capabilities
- Three AI functions: audit planning (risk assessment), execution (checklist + findings), report generation
- Multiple focused AI agents vs. single large agent
- RAG application for knowledge base queries
- Interim solution: AI agents accessing SharePoint until Snowflake ready

## Timeline (pending approval)

- Q1 2026: Feasibility and planning
- Q2 2026: Design, development, coding
- Mid-2026: Pilot deployment at select sites

## Decisions & Outcomes

- Budget approved by VP Jeff Reiberg and John Lycan
- MPL intake form completed, ready for PPG process
- Martin to review MPL form
- Decision needed before end of 2025 for 2026 planning
