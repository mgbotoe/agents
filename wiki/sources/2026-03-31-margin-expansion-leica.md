# Margin Expansion Opportunities — Leica Diagnostics

McKinsey pricing diagnostic results for Leica Biosystems. Separate workstream from the Beckman/Cepheid pricing AI work, focused specifically on Leica margin expansion.

## Key Numbers

- **$25-35M annual run-rate opportunity** identified for Leica margin expansion
- Diagnostic phase complete across Danaher diagnostics targeting $100M+ opportunity over 2 years
- Current US business: $650M revenue — this represents ~5% potential impact
- Implementation timeline: $4-5M in 2026, $14-20M in 2027, full $25-35M by 2028
- Ramp-up over 3 years due to contract lifecycles

## Priority Opportunity Areas

### Pricing & Discounting Guardrails
- Significant discount variation across customer base
- Wide dispersion between small customers (D Ryan's mold shop) vs large institutions (Duke University)
- Need better volume-based discount discipline

### Services Pricing & Attachment
- Low service attach rates despite reliable products
- Re-evaluate service tiers against market realities
- Opportunity for bundled warranty extensions

### Contract Compliance & Price Realization
- Z003 CPQ pricing pushed to SAP but not automated
- 3-5% inflationary price increases on local agreements
- Better management of contract cycles

## Specific Recommendations

- **Extended warranty bundling:** start with microtomes (high reliability, low service cost); build 3-year warranty into instrument price; removes revenue opportunity from competitors
- **Dynamic deal scoring:** upgrade existing Excel tool with analytics; reduce subjectivity; historical data-driven price recommendations
- **Long-tail SKU pricing:** current catalog 27,000+ SKUs; price floors for low-volume, high-cost-to-serve products

## Organizational Considerations

- Same team managing multiple initiatives (Deal Factory, IHC share gain)
- Colin emphasized: select 2-3 high-impact initiatives vs trying everything
- Change management critical — sales incentive alignment required
- DOA (Delegation of Authority) matrix needs updating without creating bureaucracy

## Action Items

- Colin to decide top 2-3 priorities for immediate implementation
- McKinsey to provide detailed playbook and analytics tools
- Potential continued partnership through 2026-27 implementation
- Integration with existing IHC share wallet initiatives
- Weekly/monthly KPI reporting framework to track progress

## Relationship to Pricing AI Project

This is the Leica-specific application of the broader [[projects/pricing-ai|Pricing AI (McKinsey)]] work. The $25-35M Leica opportunity is a component of the $51-71M total Danaher pricing opportunity. Dynamic deal scoring is the tool most likely to leverage AI directly.
