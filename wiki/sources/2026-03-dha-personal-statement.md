# DHA Personal Statement — Oklahoma State University

## Core Thesis
"The further I move into healthcare technology, the less I understand about the organizations my technology is supposed to serve."

## Key Points
- Career sits at intersection of healthcare and technology, but firmly on the tech side
- Leads enterprise AI strategy at Danaher — deploys AI for a company that sells TO healthcare, not one that delivers it
- Knows how to build and ship technology — doesn't know how receiving orgs make decisions, manage resources, adapt to change
- DHA closes this specific gap: payor structures, regulatory constraints, capital allocation, strategic planning
