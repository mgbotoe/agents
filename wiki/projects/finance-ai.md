# Finance AI

AI enablement for Danaher's corporate finance function. Madina driving with Siyu Wu after Ruchi shifted to AI for legal.

## Key Numbers

- $13M target potential (reference point from prior analysis)
- 460 people in corporate finance (2,200 across all finance/accounting at Danaher)
- Need $5M+ annualized ROI to be worth pursuing
- Most current use cases show low ROI ("save a few clicks daily")

## Strategy

- Map entire finance team workflow (role-based approach across 460+ people)
- Move beyond individual task improvements to systemic process redesign
- Centralize data first, then customize for specific use cases
- 80% component reuse across finance agents
- Revenue and invoice data most frequently used across all use cases
