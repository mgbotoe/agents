# CPO Mission & Vision

## Mission
WDAI doesn't just teach AI — we run on it. Product & Infrastructure exists to make that real across every team, every workflow, and every volunteer experience.

## Vision
A product ecosystem where standalone community builds naturally graduate into integrated infrastructure, reducing operational burden while scaling member impact.

## Key Principle
Our customers are the people running WDAI. Making their workflows AI-native is the product.
