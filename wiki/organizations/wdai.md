# Women Defining AI (WDAI)

Nonprofit empowering women and nonbinary individuals to lead in AI. 1,000+ members across US, Canada, UK, and EU. Founded by [[people/helen-kupp|Helen Kupp]].

## Mission
Close the gender-tech gap through curated learning programs, educational content, research, and community events. Focus on mid-career women and non-technical backgrounds.
